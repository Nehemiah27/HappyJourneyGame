Nehemiah's Happy Journey Game

Key Points

  • There is a Timer/Time Limit of 2 Minutes
  • 3 Key Constraints(discussed Briefly below)
  • Aim of the Game is to make all the Entities in the Left to be Reached to the Right

Brief Discussion


Welcome to Nehemiah's Happy Journey Game
About the Game:- There are 3 Officials and
The Officials are holding luggage
The Officials are waiting in the Lobby with their Luggage
After a day long Meeting, Officials got tired
Company managed to arrange only one Servant to help the Officials
The goal is to make the Officials reach the cab with their luggage


There are 3 constraints

Constraint no.1:
At any point of time,
Officals either in Cab/at Lobby should be lesser/equal to the Luggage available
Otherwise, Officials might think the company have impolite culture
That might also leads to an Unhappy Journey

Contraint no. 2:
The Servant can handle entities that are lesser/equal to 2
i.e., He can handle
       a. Offical+Luggage
       b. Luggage+Luggage
       c. Official+Official

Constraint no. 3:
The Servant has autophobia
So, he told the Officials that he needs atleast one entity with him
Official/Luggage in the process of helping the officials reach the cab
Is it possible for the Officals to have a Happy Journey
If yes, Can you please tell the steps to guide the Servant/Driver by playing the Game
